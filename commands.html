🌟 Reputation Bot Commands

Explore all the commands that make Reputation Bot a powerful tool for community management.

👤 User Commands

These commands are available to all users, enabling them to interact with the reputation system and track their contributions.

  • ?rep {user}: Give reputation to a user, encouraging positive interactions within the community.
  • ?myreps (only works in DM's): Check your own reputation points privately in DMs, keeping your achievements discreet.
  • ?toprep: View the top 5 users with the highest reputation in the server, recognizing the most positively engaged members.
  • ?reppoints {user}: View another user’s reputation points across all servers, fostering transparency and friendly competition.

🛠️ Admin Commands

These commands are reserved for server administrators to configure and manage the reputation system effectively.

  • /setup: Configure reputation settings, such as cooldowns and logging channels, to tailor the bot’s functionality to your server’s needs.
  • /reset_rep: with this command you can reset a users rep or your enitre servers rep. allowing more control over your servers rep.
