📖 About Reputation Bot

Reputation Bot is your go-to solution for enhancing positive interactions within your community. Designed for versatility and ease of use, this bot allows you to tailor a reputation system that aligns perfectly with your server's goals and culture.

Why Use Reputation Bot?

Encourage your members to engage in a supportive and friendly manner by rewarding positive behavior. Whether you’re managing a gaming community, a study group, or any other type of server, Reputation Bot provides the tools you need to build a thriving environment.

  • 🔄 Customizable Cooldowns: Set cooldowns for reputation commands that suit the rhythm of your community.
  • 📊 Integrated Logging: Keep track of all reputation activity with built-in logging features, ensuring transparency and accountability.
  • 🌐 Cross-Server Reputation Tracking: Easily manage and view reputation points across all servers where the bot is active, promoting unity among your communities.

✨ Key Features

🔧 Custom Cooldowns

Flexibly adjust the cooldown times for reputation commands to fit your community’s pace, allowing for a tailored experience that keeps engagement high.

🌍 Cross-Server Tracking

Effortlessly manage and view reputation points across multiple servers, providing a cohesive experience for users who participate in different communities.

Experience smooth, visually appealing embedded messages that create a professional interaction experience for your users, enhancing the overall aesthetic of your server.

🔔 Customizable Notifications

Set up notifications to keep users informed about their reputation changes, fostering an engaging environment where everyone feels recognized.
